"We didn't build a booth. We built a moment."

ComplexCon is where sneaker culture, streetwear, music, and art converge. For Adidas, we designed an activation that didn't feel like advertising — it felt like an event within the event. Custom architecture, a live DJ setup, an exclusive drop, and a social photo moment that drove 28K UGC posts over the weekend.

The activation was named Best Brand Experience at ComplexCon 2022 and drove a 14% lift in brand preference among 18–28 year-old males in the US — the most elusive demographic in the category.
